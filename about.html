Our Vision

Building the Future, One Innovation at a Time

Our goal is to democratize tech learning — one student at a time. We’re turning passive learners into confident creators by building strong foundations through mentorship and hands-on experiences.

We don’t just teach trends — we prepare the next generation of leaders and changemakers to shape the digital future.

What We Do

Hands-On Learning, Real-World Impact

From beginner-friendly coding bootcamps to AI and hardware projects, LearnIT helps students turn curiosity into capability. We organize hands-on workshops, peer-led bootcamps, mentorship programs, and collaborative sprints.

Our goal is simple: empower students to apply their classroom knowledge to solve real-world problems.

Community Impact

Our Journey

2019

The Genesis

LearnIT was founded in 2019, laying the groundwork for an ambitious tech community. The club quickly gained momentum in its first two years. During this time, we organized a national-level hackathon (like Code-e-Manipal), which was featured in a local newspaper. The IT Department was also recognized as the "Most Innovative Department."

2021

Dormant Phase

The club went through a brief period of inactivity from 2021 to 2022, largely due to the disruptions caused by the global COVID-19 pandemic. Activities slowed down as students and faculty adjusted to remote learning and lockdowns.

Down but not out, this phase became a turning point — helping us reflect, reorganize, and prepare for a stronger comeback in the years to follow.

2023

Revival & Growth

In 2023, LearnIT was revived with a new vision. The 2023–24 tenure marked the beginning of a new chapter. Highlights included a talk show with Col. Rajeev Bharwan and the hosting of DevFest, our flagship hackathon.

2024

Making Waves

The 2024–25 tenure elevated our scale and impact. We officially launched Code-e-Manipal as our flagship event, which saw over 2000 registrations and set a new benchmark for club-hosted hackathons.
